Student Online Attendance Management

The system manages student and employee attendance efficiently by recording exact entry and exit times.

Timeline
Sept 2022 – Dec 2022
Role
Undergraduate Researcher
Stack
python, c++, ml
Contributions
Reporting & Notifications – Generates detailed reports and alerts for absentees or late arrivals.

Approach

  • Manual Login-Based – Students mark attendance by logging in to the system.
  • RFID Card-Based – Attendance recorded automatically when students swipe RFID cards.
  • Biometric-Based – Fingerprint or facial recognition used to verify identity and mark attendance.
  • GPS/Location-Based – Mobile app tracks student presence using GPS location within campus.
  • Reporting & Notifications – Generates detailed reports and alerts for absentees or late arrivals.

Highlights

  • Real-time attendance tracking and reporting.
  • Automated notifications for absentees or late arrivals.
  • Secure, centralized, and accessible online from anywhere.
