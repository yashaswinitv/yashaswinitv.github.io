scientometric-analysis of machine learning

Built an end-to-end scientometric analysis pipeline to examine the structural,thematic and temporal evolution of ml research. Delivered reproducible workflows and data-driven insights to support strategic academic and industry research decisions.

Timeline
May 2025 – Sep 2025
Role
Graduate Researcher
Stack
Python, TensorFlow, NumPy, SciPy, Matplotlib
Contributions
Signal preprocessing, CNN architecture, evaluation, reporting

Approach

  • Curated and cleaned large-scale bibilometric datasets to isolate machine learning across journals and conferences.
  • Constructed co-authoeship and citation network to quantify influence and collabration pattern using graph centrality metrics.
  • Architected a multi-branch CNN with residual connections to capture temporal patterns and reduce overfitting on noisy inputs.
  • Applied topic modelling LDA and keywors co-occurance analysis to uncover emerging ml subfields and thematic shifts.

Highlights

  • Identified the post-2012 deep learning surge as a structural inflection point in publication and citation accleration.
  • Mapped high-centrality researchers and institutions,revealing dominant collaboration clusters and interdisciplinary bridges.
  • Delivered a narrative report translating complex model outputs into actionable insights for technical work.
